# Dataset for: Atomistic Origins of Tension-Compression Asymmetry and Optoelectronic Enhancement in Hybrid Perovskites

This repository contains the dataset supporting the research article: **"Atomistic Origins of Tension-Compression Asymmetry and Optoelectronic Enhancement in Hybrid Perovskites"** submitted to *Computational Materials Science*.

## 📌 Overview
In this study, first-principles calculations based on Density Functional Theory (DFT) were employed to investigate the directional mechanical behaviors and optoelectronic coupling of the tetragonal methylammonium lead iodide (MAPbI3) perovskite under continuous loading. 

The fundamental data dictating all the findings in our manuscript—including energy evolution, stress-strain curves, electronic band structures, Rashba splitting, and carrier effective masses—are the relaxed unit cell structural models under different strains. To ensure full transparency and reproducibility, we provide the fully relaxed structure files (`CONTCAR` files generated by VASP) for both tensile and compressive deformation processes along the [1 0 0] crystallographic direction.
